# Microsoft 365 Business Premium Now Available for GCC High
The launch of Microsoft 365 Business Premium for GCC High marks a significant milestone for government contractors and organizations handling sensitive data in highly regulated environments. With this new offering, Microsoft extends its enterprise-grade security and compliance features to businesses with up to 500 users, providing a tailored cloud solution designed to meet the stringent requirements of the U.S. government and Defense Industrial Base.

## What Is GCC High?
GCC High is a specialized cloud environment designed exclusively for U.S. federal agencies, defense contractors, and organizations working with Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI), ITAR, and other sensitive government data. It offers physical data isolation within U.S. borders and restricts access to vetted U.S. persons, ensuring compliance with the most rigorous government security standards. This environment is governed by strict cybersecurity mandates, including the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC), which contractors must adhere to in order to participate in government contracts.

## Why Business Premium Matters for GCC High
Previously, small and midsize businesses working under these regulations often faced challenges balancing cost, complexity, and compliance. Many had to opt for larger enterprise plans like Microsoft 365 E5, which, while comprehensive, could be financially and operationally burdensome. The introduction of Business Premium for GCC High changes this by offering a cost-effective, streamlined solution tailored for organizations with fewer than 500 seats, without compromising on security or compliance capabilities.

## Key Features
- Access to familiar Microsoft 365 apps (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).

- Advanced security features designed for government regulations.

- Compliance tools to streamline CMMC, ITAR, and other mandates.

- Secure data storage and sharing limited strictly to other GCC High and DoD tenants.

- Enterprise-grade protections, including Microsoft Defender and Information Protection.​

- Designed and priced for organizations with fewer than 500 seats.

## Compliance and Security Benefits
This new plan empowers smaller organizations in the defense supply chain to comply with federal cybersecurity mandates cost-effectively. It provides a trusted platform where sensitive government data can be securely accessed, shared, and protected, reducing risk while meeting regulatory requirements. For many, Microsoft 365 Business Premium for GCC High is the crucial link that allows them to confidently compete for government contracts without sacrificing security or compliance.

## Microsoft 365 G5 Security Add-on
The G5 Security add-on enhances Microsoft 365 Business Premium for GCC High by introducing advanced threat protection and unified security management capabilities designed for government-level defense against sophisticated cyber threats. Key features include:

Microsoft Defender for Endpoint: Provides endpoint detection and response (EDR), proactive threat hunting, and automated investigation and remediation capabilities.

Defender for Office 365 Plan 2: Advanced protection against phishing, business email compromise, and zero-day malware through real-time detection.

Microsoft Cloud App Security: Visibility and control over cloud applications with threat detection and policies to protect sensitive data.

Microsoft Defender for Identity: Monitors and detects identity-based threats across on-premises Active Directory and cloud environments.

This add-on is crucial for organizations requiring heightened security posture beyond baseline protections, helping safeguard sensitive government data against evolving cyberattacks and insider threats.

## Microsoft 365 G5 Compliance Add-on
The G5 Compliance add-on extends compliance capabilities within the GCC High environment, enabling organizations to meet rigorous regulatory frameworks such as DoD standards and CMMC requirements more effectively. It includes:

Advanced eDiscovery: Facilitates complex investigations, legal hold, and audit trails tailored for regulated environments.

Insider Risk Management: Detects and manages internal risks, such as data leaks or policy violations, with automated workflows and alerts.

Information Governance: Provides tools for data lifecycle management, including retention policies and records management aligned with government standards.

Communication Compliance: Monitors organizational communications for policy adherence and regulatory compliance risks.

This add-on helps organizations maintain compliance posture while automating costly and complex manual processes related to data governance and regulatory audits.

## Combined Impact
When combined, the G5 Security and Compliance add-ons transform Microsoft 365 Business Premium for GCC High into a comprehensive, enterprise-grade cloud platform. This toolkit supports not just productivity but also the advanced security and compliance frameworks mandated by federal regulations. It empowers government contractors—especially those under 500 users—to maintain secure operations in a cost-effective manner while confidently addressing cybersecurity mandates.
